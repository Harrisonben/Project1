The Wizard's Mind Bomb

Its taste has been compared to warm tap water with a lot of fizz

When you need that little extra boost of magic experience to enter the prestigous mage guild

Ingredients

  • 2 Buckets of water
  • 8 ounces of Barley malt
  • 12 ounces of Yanillian hops
  • one pot of ale yeast
  • Beer glass

Recipe Instructions

  1. Use 2 buckets of water on the ferementing vat
  2. 2 barley malt on the fermenting vat
  3. add 12 ounces of Yanillian hops to the vat
  4. use ale yeast on the fermenting vat
  5. wait about 2 days for the beer to ferment
  6. Enjoy!
